THE PROGRAMME

Where the archive meets the dancefloor.

RAWA takes its name from a Hausa word meaning 'dance.' Every edition is curated around the music that shaped Nigerian culture across three decades — the afrobeat, jùjú, highlife, and early pop that filled rooms from Lagos to Kaduna long before the internet existed.

Each night is built on one principle: if the music is good enough to have survived forty years, it's good enough to hold a room tonight.

RAWA launched in 2022 and has run without interruption since — across the French Institute, Ekondo, Jabi Boat Club, and Pickle Social House.
