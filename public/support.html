GET INVOLVED

Support the Work

Afrosonic runs on the energy of people who believe Nigerian music heritage is worth protecting. If that includes you, there are several ways to be part of what we're building.

Looking to donate vinyl records or cassettes? Visit the Archive page →

VOLUNTEER

We need people, not just money.

Afrosonic runs with a small core team and a group of volunteers who show up each month and make the events work. Volunteering means being part of something that is genuinely being built.

  • — Event support — setup, guest management, breakdown at RAWA and Film Club
  • — Vinyl stand — curating and managing the vinyl display at RAWA
  • — Photography and documentation — capturing events for the archive and social media
  • — Writing — contributing to Afrosonic Magazine

All volunteers receive a briefing before each event, a named role, and a stipend for their time. We do not ask people to work for free.

Express your interest → contact@afrosonic.org
DONATE FINANCIALLY
