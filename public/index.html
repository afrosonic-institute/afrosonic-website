WHAT WE DO
01

RAWA

Vintage Nigerian music. Live. Every month.

A monthly DJ night bringing heritage Afro-pop and Nigerian music from the 1970s to the early 2000s to a new generation of listeners. Eleven editions. Four Abuja venues. One unbroken argument: this music was never supposed to disappear.

02

Film Club

African cinema. Once a month.

Monthly film screening and discussion exploring African stories in the context of the music and culture that shaped them. In partnership with Alien Prose Studios, Abuja.

FROM THE ARCHIVE

Still Searching: Afrosonic meets Bongos Ikwue

"The day you try to change yourself, I think you're gone — because you can only be you."

— Bongos Ikwue, speaking to Afrosonic

We spent an afternoon at the home of Bongos Ikwue — composer, bandleader, and one of the most original voices in Nigerian music history. He talked about growing up in Jos, the Groovy Train, what it means to write a song from a dream, and sixty years of staying exactly himself.
