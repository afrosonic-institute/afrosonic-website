THE PROGRAMME

The context behind the music.

Nigerian heritage music does not exist in a vacuum. It was made during specific moments — political, social, creative — that shaped what the music said and how it sounded. The Film Club exists to bring those moments into view.

Each month, Afrosonic Film Club screens one film chosen for its relationship to the African cultural and musical landscape. After the screening, the room stays open for discussion.

In partnership with Alien Prose Studios, Abuja.
